All about the Game

Selecting Empty Board gives you an clear empty board to start with. The 6x6 game board starts with an random alphabet choosen from a random word. The first cell of the first row and first column is filled with the random alphabet. The random word choosen shall be displayed on top right, ex:- Hint : potato. Random alphabet 'o' is placed on the first cell. Keyboard Arrow keys can be used for moving the alphabet to the down row. All you have to do is remember the index of the alphabet from the word and place it in its respective place down to the bottom row. the Hint updates as and when the alphabet is placed in the bottom row showing the remaining alphabets in the word. Once a word is placed in the correct manner it will be cleared and score updated and ready for the next word. Wrong placements fill up the board and once the board is filled up before a targeted score you shall have option to swap the alphabets to form proper words. Game shall be over either by achieving the target score or by clearing most of the board.

Selecting Random Board starts with a completly filled board with alphabets choosen from random words, which are filled randomly across the board. Start swapping the alphabets to form a proper word, which clears the word and again filed with random alpahbets, till the targeted score is achieved. Game shall be over by acheiving the target score.
